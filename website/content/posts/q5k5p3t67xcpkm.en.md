### Q&A: Honest Answers to Your Concerns
**Q1: Won't the generated script just make things up (hallucinations)?**
A sharp observation. That is exactly why you shouldn't leave everything to the AI. The standard approach is to integrate a "RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)" structure, where you call a reliable news source via API and hand that data to the AI as a "teleprompter." Let the AI handle the "narration" while the external source guarantees the "facts." This is the mature way to manage the relationship.

**Q2: What about copyright and monetization hurdles?**
Approach this carefully but boldly. If you select APIs that allow commercial use, monetization via YouTube or podcasts is well within reach. Check the terms of service, and play the AI as your strongest piece within the rules of the game.

**Q3: Won't the pronunciation of technical terms be a disaster?**
